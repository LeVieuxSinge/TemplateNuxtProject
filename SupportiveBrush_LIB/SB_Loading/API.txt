SB_Loading v1.0 (April 2019)
Designed by SupportiveBrush (Xavier Champoux)

1. Add 'SB_Loading.css' or 'SB_Loading.scss' to your HTML.
2. Add 'SB_Loading.js' to your HTML.
3. Add the following code to your body:

Example Code:

    <div class="sb-loading">
        <div class="sb-loading-text sb-loading-text-hidden">
            <h1 class="sb-loading-time"></h1>
        </div>
        <div class="sb-loading-slide sb-loading-slide-1">
            <h1 class="sb-loading-time"></h1>
        </div>
        <div class="sb-loading-slide sb-loading-slide-2">
            <h1 class="sb-loading-time"></h1>
        </div>
        <div class="sb-loading-slide sb-loading-slide-3">
            <h1 class="sb-loading-time"></h1>
        </div>
        <div class="sb-loading-slide sb-loading-slide-4">
            <h1 class="sb-loading-time"></h1>
        </div>
    </div>

4. If you wish to keep the font, copy paste it from 'reset.css'. *Open Sans Condensed from Google Fonts*
5. Call SB_LoadingDone() once your desired elements all fully loaded.
